1. Site Name:

Jolley Supreme Games

I named the website Jolley Supreme Games because I love video games and Jolley Supreme has always been a name that I've used.

2. Site Purpose:

The site purpose is to help people get into video games without wasting time or money on games they might not like. This site will help people choose which games they should consider buying and which ones will fit their wants more.

3. Scenarios:

What if someone has a little bit of extra time on their hands and wants to start gaming but doesn't want to play a game with the little time that they do have that they are not going to enjoy? That's where we come in!

What if someone loves to play games but has never got into playing video games? What if they don't know where to start and don't want to spend days looking at different gaming review to decide on what game to start with?
